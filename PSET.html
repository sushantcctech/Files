Why PSET APIs? "How ObjectARX will bridge engagements between CCTech and Autodesk?"

The Autodesk PSET is leading this future with a cloud-first evolution in design and make software. Now teams across ecosystems and industries can create together in a way that is exponentially smarter, faster and more impactful. CCTech has a longstanding and deeper ties with Autodesk on many fronts hence it is important for us to be adept in all the technologies leveraged by Autodesk for deeper engagements and connections. Autodesk PSET is a set of APIs and web services that you can use to build innovative cloud-powered applications. Today companies are using Autodesk PSET to build digital twins, visualize and analyze data, render photos to 3D, integrate AR and VR, create product catalogs and Configurators, automate design processes, and integrate SaaS products.

The outcomes can be in the form of applications, plugins, libraries, and many more such things. With this context being set it is necessary for CCTech to have Developers equipped with this skill-set which will not only help them establish their own connection with Autodesk but also boost their careers in that front.
